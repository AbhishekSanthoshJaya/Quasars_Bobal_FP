WHAT WE DO

bobal is a unique service that allows users to meet people over their favorite sports and games. How it works is, one user books a venue for a particular game and then other users can join that game. We value your time so we've created a way for users to enjoy their favorite game without the hassle of waiting for venues to be free! Book. Meet. Play. It's that simple.
